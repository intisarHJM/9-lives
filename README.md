# 9-Lives
A 2D side-scrolling runner game where a black cat must dodge falling obstacles — sandals and trash bags — raining from the sky, testing how many lives it can keep out of nine.

## Starting Date: 5/8/2026
## Completion Date: 7/8/2026
### By: Intisar Abdulla

### ***Description***
#### 9-Lives is a browser-based endless runner built with Phaser 4. The player controls a black cat sprinting through a city street while dodging obstacles falling from above. Every hit costs one of the cat's nine lives, and the score climbs the longer the cat survives.
#### with the challenge escalating once the score crosses 500, as trash bags join the sandals in the sky.

### ***Getting Started***
1. Clone the repository and run `npm install` to install dependencies.
2. Run `npm start` to launch the Parcel dev server, then open the game in your browser.
3. On the Title Screen, press **SPACE** (or click) to start the game.
4. Use the **Left/Right Arrow Keys** to move the cat across the street.
5. Press **SPACE** to jump over or dodge falling obstacles.
6. Avoid the sandals falling from the sky — each hit reduces your remaining lives, shown at the top of the screen alongside your current score.
7. Once your score passes 500, trash bags start falling too, adding a faster, tougher obstacle to watch for.
8. If the cat is hit 9 times, the Game Over screen appears showing your final score.
9. Press **SPACE** on the Game Over screen to restart and try to beat your score.
